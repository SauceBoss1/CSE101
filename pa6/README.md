# Programming Assignment 6:
## Calculating Big Integers (C++)

### Description/Synopsis:
This program takes a Big Integer (BI) file and does arithmetic on those BIs.
The following is the calculations that are outputted:
- $$A$$
- $$B$$
- $$ A + B $$
- $$ A - B $$
- $$ A - A $$
- $$ 3A - 2B $$
- $$ AB $$
- $$ A^2 $$
- $$ B^2 $$
- $$ 9A^4 + 16B^5 $$
